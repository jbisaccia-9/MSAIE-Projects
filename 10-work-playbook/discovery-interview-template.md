## Warm-up (5 min)
- Walk me through yesterday morning, start to lunch.
- What part of the week do you dread?

## The four gold veins (20 min)
1. **Repetition:** What do you type/write/fill out over and over?
   What's the template-that-isn't-a-template?
2. **Lookup drudgery:** What do you have to go *find* constantly?
   Where do you look? How often is the answer stale or missing?
   Do you keep a personal cheat sheet? *(If yes: can I see it? — that
   document is worth more than the official SOPs.)*
3. **Handoffs:** Where do things sit waiting on another person/department?
   What gets lost or re-asked in the handoff?
4. **Rework:** What comes back to be redone? Why?

## Reality checks (10 min)
- If I gave you 5 hours back a week, what would you spend them on?
  *(Tests whether the pain is real — vague answer = weak pain.)*
- What tools/systems do you touch daily? Which one do you fight with?
- Are you already using any AI tools for work, officially or not?
  *(Amnesty framing: "totally fine either way — I'm mapping, not policing.")*
- Who else should I talk to?
