> **"I run an AI engineering program inside a HIPAA healthcare org — I find
> the pain points, build and evaluate the agents, train the staff, and answer

resumes; "deployed org-wide under HIPAA with an eval suite and a governance
register" is maybe ten. Healthcare AI is your moat now (fintech demoted to
secondary angle — the trading agent still tells it).

The role is broad (deploy + train + advise). Breadth without the deep-build
muscle plateaus at "AI coordinator." The build track (modules 01–09) is what
keeps you an *engineer* who can move anywhere — that's why the 7am blocks
survive the new job, not despite it.
